Practice Problem 2.4
====================


Question
--------

Give the answers in hexidecimal:

* A: 0x503C + 0x8 = ?
* B: 0x503C - 0x40 = ?
* C: 0x503C + 64 = ?
* D: 0x50EA - 0x503c = ?


Answer
------

* A: 0x503C + 0x8 = 0x5044
* B: 0x503C - 0x40 = 0x4FFC
* C: 0x503C + 64 = 0x507C
* D: 0x50EA - 0x503c = 0xAE
